Facility Information

PowerPlayz Futsal is the premier indoor soccer facility in Kota Kinabalu, featuring 4 state-of-the-art FIFA-approved turf courts.

Amenities

  • ✓ Air-conditioned courts
  • ✓ Men's & Women's showers
  • ✓ Public prayer room (surau)
  • ✓ Free high-speed WiFi
  • ✓ Vending machines

Rules & Regulations

  1. All players must wear non-marking indoor court shoes. No studs allowed.
  2. Bookings must be cancelled at least 24 hours in advance for a full refund.
  3. No outside food or drink allowed (water bottles are okay).
  4. Smoking is strictly prohibited on the premises.
